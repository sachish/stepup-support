FAQ

How do I allow step tracking?
iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness → enable StepUp.

Why is my step count not updating?
Try opening StepUp and tapping Refresh. Make sure Motion permission is enabled.

How do goals work?
Set a daily target and track your progress each day.
